NAVIGATING SPACE

DAY 1

TIME: 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM

SPEAKER:MR. SHIBU MATHEW

workshop agenda

  • Join Mr. Shibu Mathew, Division Head at ISRO's ERFD, for an immersive exploration of liquid propulsion in space missions. The agenda will cover a comprehensive overview of liquid propulsion systems, focusing on advancements and innovations from recent landmark missions such as Chandrayaan 3, Aditya LI, and Gangayan.
  • Sessions will delve into the design principles, operational challenges, and future prospects of liquid propulsion technology, including propulsion systems for lunar exploration, solar observation, and interplanetary missions. Participants will also engage in interactive discussions, case studies, and Q&A sessions with Mr. Mathew and fellow experts to gain deeper insights into the cutting-edge realm of liquid propulsion.

workshop features

  • This workshop offers an unparalleled opportunity to learn from the forefront of space exploration, with Mr. Shibu Mathew providing firsthand insights from leading ISRO missions. Participants will benefit from a blend Of theoretical knowledge and practical experience, with real-world examples drawn from Chandrayaan 3, Aditya LI, and Gangayan missions. Interactive elements include simulations, demonstrations, and hands-on activities to enhance understanding and foster collaboration among attendees. Additionally, participants will gain access to exclusive resources and networking opportunities to further enrich their understanding of liquid propulsion technology and its applications in space exploration.
