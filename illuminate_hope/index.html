ILLUMINATE HOPE

DAY 1

TIME: 01:30 PM - 02:30 PM

SPEAKER:MR. ABHINAV RAJEEV

workshop agenda

  • The workshop led by Abhinav Rajeev will commence with an introduction to solar energy and its applications, followed by a hands-on session on assembling solar lamps. Participants will be guided through the process of constructing 25 solar lamps using provided materials. Abhinav will share insights on the importance of sustainable energy solutions, particularly in rural areas prone to power cuts, and how these lamps can positively impact the lives of students.
  • The workshop will conclude with a discussion on the distribution and implementation of the lamps, ensuring they reach the intended beneficiaries effectively.

workshop features

  • Participants will benefit from Abhinav Rajeev's expertise as a Research Fellow at IIT Palakkad and Secretary of IEEE SIGHT Kerala Section, gaining valuable insights into solar energy and its practical applications. The workshop offers a unique hands-on experience, allowing participants to directly contribute to social impact by crafting solar lamps to brighten the lives of rural students facing electricity challenges. Through this interactive session, attendees will not only acquire technical skills in assembling solar lamps but also develop a deeper understanding of the importance of sustainable energy solutions and community empowerment.
