EMPOWER FROM WITHIN

DAY 1

TIME: 01:30 PM - 02:30 PM

SPEAKER:PROF. SUNITHA BEEVI

workshop agenda

  • IJoin us for a dynamic session on women's empowerment led by Prof. Sunitha Beevi, Chair of IEEE SIGHT Kerala Section. The workshop will commence with an introduction to key issues facing women today, followed by Prof. Beevi's expert insights on strategies for empowerment. Participants will engage in interactive discussions on fostering inclusivity, leveraging technology for social impact, and building supportive networks.
  • Additionally, breakout sessions will offer practical tools and resources for driving positive change in communities. The session will conclude with a collaborative action planning session, empowering attendees to implement tangible initiatives for advancing gender equality and empowerment.

workshop features

  • This workshop offers a unique opportunity to gain actionable insights and practical tools for driving positive change in your community. Led by Prof. Sunitha Beevi, a renowned expert in women's empowerment and technology, participants will benefit from engaging discussions, interactive activities, and valuable networking opportunities. Through a blend of expert-led presentations, interactive discussions, and hands-on breakout sessions, attendees will leave empowered with the knowledge, skills, and inspiration to make a meaningful impact in advancing gender equality and women's empowerment in their communities
